About Me

I'm Navjot Kaur, a passionate young filmmaker and student from India. My journey in filmmaking began with a simple curiosity about stories that matter, and has grown into a deep commitment to using the camera as a tool for social change.

Growing up in a culturally rich environment, I've always been fascinated by human emotions and the power of visual storytelling. From documenting community initiatives to exploring complex social issues, my work focuses on giving voice to the unheard.
